Benefits of Integration of Square and Amazon

  • Real-Time Inventory Management

    One of the major benefits of the integration of Square as well as Amazon is real-time inventory sync. When a client buys an item from Amazon stock levels will be instantly adjusted within Square POS. In contrast, purchases made in stores are reflected on the Amazon marketplace. This stops overselling and stockouts, making sure customers get accurate availability information.

    Order fulfillment is more efficient when marketplace integration is integrated. Retailers can monitor and control all Amazon orders via the Square dashboard.
